More about the platform
Make journeys make sense

Advertising reinvented

illumin revolutionizes online advertising with the world’s first journey advertising platform, aligning seamlessly with the way marketers think. Our technology empowers you to create, activate, and optimize campaigns across all your channels (and the entire marketing funnel), backed by real-time insights for maximum control and client support.

Email integration

Sync first-party email audiences

illumin is taking connected journey advertising to the next level with the first fully connected email audience integration. Our intuitive and easy-to-use interface connects to your email marketing platform to seamlessly sync first-party audiences to open web campaigns based on user behavior such as opens, clicks, delivered, and unsubscribes.
